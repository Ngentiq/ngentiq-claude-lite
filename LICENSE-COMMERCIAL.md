## Ownership Model

This project follows a **three-tier ownership structure**:

| Tier      | Location                                              | Ownership            |
|-----------|-------------------------------------------------------|----------------------|
| Managed   | `.claude/sdlc/`, `.claude/skills/`, `.claude/settings.json` (framework entries) | Licensed (ngentiq)   |
| Customer  | `.claude/PROJECT-RULES.md`, `.claude/agents/`, project `CLAUDE.md` | Your Organization    |
| User      | `.claude/settings.local.json`                         | Individual Users     |

**Important:**
All customer-created configurations, agents, rules, and artifacts are **fully owned by the customer**.
No ownership is claimed over customer or user content.
